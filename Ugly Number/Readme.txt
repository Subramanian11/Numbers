Ugly Numbers are the numbers which are only divisible by the prime numbers 2,3 and 5. 
The number should not be divisible by any other number.

Example:

Input: N = 14 
Output: No 
Explanation: 
14 is not ugly since it includes another prime factor 7.

Input: N = 6 
Output: Yes 
Explanation: 
6 is a ugly since it includes 2 and 3.

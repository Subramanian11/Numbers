The number divisible by 1 and itself is considered as a prime number. 
Number of factors will be 2 for prime numbers.

Example:

input:7
output:Prime number
explanation: 7 is divisible by 1 and 7. Not divisible by any other number. so it is a prime number.

input:8
output: Not a Prime number
explanation: 8 is divisible by 1,2,4 and 8. So it is not a prime number.
